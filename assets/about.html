What is Veguto?

Veguto is a simple corner of the internet created to be a home for all things cute and wholesome. The main goal of this website is to share the things I create—whether they are small coding experiments or light interactive games—away from the complexity of larger platforms.

The Content

Here, you will find a collection of simple games and projects characterized by a cheerful and gentle spirit. Since cats are the most wonderful and soft creatures, they are the heart of most experiences shared here. Through this humble content, Veguto hopes to succeed in drawing a gentle smile on your face and offering you a few peaceful, happy moments. 🐾
